The Concept

A salad bar that treats vegetables like the main event — not the side you settle for.

What started as a small salad counter grew, one bowl at a time, into a full kitchen covering brunch, pasta, burgers and dessert — without ever losing the produce-first instinct that started it all. Every plate still begins the same way: fresh vegetables, prepared daily, arranged with care.

Today we serve dine-in, take-out and delivery, six days a week, from our kitchen.

From the Kitchen

A closer look.

A glimpse of what leaves our kitchen every day — shared straight from our counter.

We don't chase trends. We chase the vegetable that's ripest this week — and build the plate around it.

Behind the counter, our kitchen preps every base, sauce and dressing from scratch, every single service day. Nothing sits pre-made in a walk-in for a week — it's cut, cooked and dressed the day it reaches your table, whether you're dining in, picking up, or ordering delivery.

That discipline is the whole reason a salad counter has grown, six years on, into a full kitchen — and it's still the standard we hold ourselves to on every single order.
